Top Destinations

Sigiriya

Climb the iconic Lion Rock and explore ancient frescoes.

Ella

Experience Stunning Hikes, Waterfalls, and Tea Plantations in Sri Lanka’s Central Highlands

Sri Pada (Adam's Peak)

Experience the mystical allure of Adam's Peak, a sacred mountain with a footprint-shaped rock formation.

Kandy

The cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and serene lake views.

Anuradhapura

An ancient city filled with majestic stupas, sacred Bodhi trees, and a rich history of Sri Lankan Buddhism.

Galle

A coastal city famous for its Dutch colonial architecture and the iconic Galle Fort.

Nuwara Eliya

Known as 'Little England,' a hill station with lush tea plantations and cool, misty weather.

Mirissa

A beach paradise perfect for whale watching, surfing, and relaxing in a tropical setting.

Yala National Park

A premier wildlife destination offering safaris to spot leopards, elephants, and diverse bird species.
